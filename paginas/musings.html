group dinner blessing

I spent the weekend 2 hours north of NYC, in a little farm town called North Salem. At Saturday night dinner, a friend, Lily, shared a blessing before the meal that I loved and want to integrate into my life on a consistent basis:

For food in a world where many walk in hunger,
For friends in world where many walk alone,
And for love in a world where many walk in fear,
We are ever grateful.
